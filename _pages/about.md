Selected projects
======

### Interactive relativity simulations

I have built interactive simulations for teaching special relativity, including VPython visualizations of Lorentz contraction, Minkowski diagrams, and Michelson interferometry.

### CHSH Bell inequality simulation

I implemented a simulation of the CHSH Bell inequality test using Qiskit, including Bell-state preparation, randomized measurement bases, correlation calculations, and visualization of results.

### E91 quantum key distribution

I worked on an implementation of the E91 quantum key distribution protocol using entangled Bell pairs, CHSH testing, key extraction, and message encryption/decryption.
